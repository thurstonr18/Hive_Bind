Welcome to the Hive

Hive Bind is a remote device management platform designed with IoT in mind that makes monitoring, management, and pushing software updates easier than ever.
Add Upload Edit Assign Push Monitor
All it takes to add your device to the hive is the simple running of a script. You can download this script from the hardware page.

After installation completes, you will find your device in the hardware console.
Upload code and create Software Groups for which to assign your devices. Let us keep track of your hardware so you can focus on software.
Edit the software that you upload.
You read that correctly.
Not sure about a line of code? Want to change something on the fly without re-uploading?
You can open your own software in a rich text editor with preserved whitespace and escape characters.
When you're satisfied with your software, just set up a dedicated group and assign devices to that group. You can even see which and how many devices are not currently bound to a group.
Sending your updated software over-the-air only takes about a minute or two. You will be notified once the push has completed both on individual devices and in the group's entirety.
Each device you bind will be given its own detailed page. You can get there by clicking the device's friendly name. From there you will find information about that device, as well as neighboring devices on its network.
